# TypeScript Quiz

* * *

You can test your TypeScript skills with W3Schools' Quiz.

* * *

## The Test

The test contains 25 questions and there is no time limit.

The test is not official, it's just a nice way to see how much you know, or don't know, about TypeScript.

## Count Your Score

You will get 1 point for each correct answer.

At the end of the Quiz, your total score will be displayed. Maximum score is 25 points.

## Start the Quiz

Good luck!

[Start the TypeScript Quiz ❯](https://www.w3schools.com/quiztest/quiztest.php?qtest=TS)

If you don't know TypeScript, we suggest that you read our [TypeScript Tutorial](index.php.html) from scratch.
